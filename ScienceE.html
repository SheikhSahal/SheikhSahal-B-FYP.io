Earth

1) Earth is the third planet from the sun in our solar system. Its name comes from the old English and Germanic words meaning 'the ground'. 2) Our amazing planet has been around for quite some time. By researching our planet's rocks, scientists have calculated the Earth to be around 4.5 billion years old! 3) Till now earth is the only planet where we can find living things such as humans, animal, nature (plants). While large volumes of water can be found throughout the Solar System, only Earth sustains liquid surface water. About 71% of Earth's surface is made up of the ocean, dwarfing Earth's polar ice, lakes, and rivers.
