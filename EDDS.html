DAILY ROUTINE FOR A SCHOOL KID.

  • 7:30 am: Get up early in the morning.
  • 7:40 am: Wash your face/take bath, brush your teeth, get school dress, and put on shoes.
  • 8:00 am: Eat breakfast. (Always wash hands before and after eating anything).
  • 8:20 am: Put your lunch in the bag and looks at books while you get ready to go.
  • 1:00 pm: Coming back from School.
  • 1:05 pm: Put the bag on the table, remove shoes and put them in shoe rack, change the school dress and put it in wardrobe or if washable/dirty put it in laundry.
  • 01:30 pm: Eat your lunch.
  • 01:45 pm: Take some rest. Approx. an hour.
  • 02:45 pm: After getting rest wash your face and be ready for the rest of day.
  • 03:00 pm: Do some exercise for fresh and healthy body.
  • 04:00 pm: Do your homework and repeat the lessons of today.
  • 05:30 pm: Go out for fresh air and enjoy with your friends.
  • 06:30 pm: Make sure to prepare your school bag for tomorrow put all books in bag, brush your shoes.
  • 07:00 pm: Help mom in preparing dinner.
  • 07:30 pm: Have dinner on time.
  • 8:00 pm: Set your bed alarm and put things on place.
  • 8:15 pm: Brush your teeth.
  • 08:30 pm: Lay on bed for sleep.
