Tenses

Present Perfect Tense:

01

Definition

In present perfect, the action is complete or has ended and hence termed Perfect.

02

Affirmative

Subject + has/have + 3rd form of verb + object
I have finished my homework
She has passed the examination

03

Negative

Subject+ has/have+ not+ 3rd form of verb + Object.
I have not finished my homework
She has not passed the examination

04

Interrogative

Has/Have + Subject + 3rd form of verb +Object?
Have you finished your homework?
Has she passed the examination?
